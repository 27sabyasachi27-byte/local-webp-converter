Why use LocalWebP for Image Conversion?

LocalWebP is designed for privacy-conscious users and professionals who need to convert images without risking data leaks. Unlike standard converters that upload your photos to a cloud server, LocalWebP processes everything inside your web browser.

  • Zero Server Uploads: Your images never leave your device.
  • Unlimited Batching: Convert hundreds of images at once.
  • Offline Capable: Works without an internet connection once loaded.
  • Free Forever: No premium tiers or hidden costs.

How to Convert JPG/PNG to WebP in Bulk

  1. Drag & Drop: Drag your folder of JPG or PNG files into the "Upload Images" zone above.
  2. Adjust Quality: Use the "Compression Quality" slider to balance file size and image fidelity (Default is 80%).
  3. Start Conversion: Click the green "Start Conversion" button.
  4. Download: Click "Download All" to save a ZIP file of your optimized WebP images.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is LocalWebP free?
Yes, LocalWebP is 100% free to use for both personal and commercial projects. There are no daily limits.
Are my photos uploaded to a server?
No. LocalWebP runs entirely in your web browser using HTML5 Canvas technology. Your images are processed on your computer's CPU and never touch our servers.
Does this work offline?
Yes! Once the page is loaded, you can disconnect from the internet and continue converting images securely.
